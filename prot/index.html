Advocate Durvijay Singh

Durvijay Singh is a distinguished Advocate practicing before the Allahabad High Court with decades of courtroom experience and an unwavering commitment to justice.

Beginning his legal journey in 1994 and officially commencing practice in 1996, he has established a reputation for integrity, strategic litigation, and dedicated legal representation.

Over the years, he has successfully represented clients in numerous criminal, civil, constitutional, family, and property matters while maintaining the highest standards of professional ethics.
